# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: Bridging the Digital Brain and the Physical Body

Welcome to the comprehensive course on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This curriculum is designed to take you from foundational concepts to advanced implementations in embodied artificial intelligence.

## Course Overview

This course combines theoretical understanding with practical applications, focusing on:

- **Embodied Intelligence**: How AI systems can interact with the physical world
- **Robot Operating System (ROS)**: Framework for developing robotic applications
- **Digital Twins**: Simulated environments for robotics development
- **AI-Robot Integration**: Connecting large language models and AI systems with robotic hardware
- **Humanoid Platforms**: Specialized approaches for bipedal robot control
